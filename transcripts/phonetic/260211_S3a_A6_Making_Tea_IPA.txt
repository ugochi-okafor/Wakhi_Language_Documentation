# Phonetic Transcription Example

Language:
Wakhi

Text:
A6 - Making Tea

Note:

The IPA transcription represents the analysed segment from the fieldwork assignment. Some forms were revised after repeated listening and discussion because the initial transcription contained uncertainties caused by recording conditions and keyboard noise.


Sentence 1

Lexical line:

ska ragnig-eʃ zerdig-ʃ ska din ha yuk-ʃ ra kadin


IPA:

[transcription example retained from fieldwork analysis]


Translation:

You put the pot on the fire and pour water in.


---

Sentence 2

Lexical line:

aval dangk-ti tʃai ra kadin


Translation:

First, put the tea in the pot.


---

Sentence 3

Lexical line:

yan-iʃ woz zak liter-in mat vita zak ha peʃt


Translation:

And then it is left for a little while and it boils.


---

Sentence 4

Lexical line:

pisak ʃiru vitja


Translation:

Then the cooking starts.


---

Sentence 5

Lexical line:

yadge-ʃ ra kadin zak woz padze


Translation:

There you pour in a little more milk and let it cook.


---

Sentence 6

Lexical line:

yan-iʃ tʃai teor wost


Translation:

Then the tea is ready.


# Transcription Notes

The first IPA attempt was reviewed during the project.

Main learning points:

- phonetic transcription should be clearly separated from lexical representation
- repeated listening is required for accurate speech analysis
- unclear segments should remain marked as uncertain rather than over-interpreted
