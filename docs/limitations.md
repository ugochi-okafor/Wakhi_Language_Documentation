# Limitations

## 1. Speaker Coverage
Only one consultant was used. This limits:
- dialect variation
- generalisability
- sociolinguistic comparison

---

## 2. Data Type Bias
Most data comes from:
- elicitation prompts
- controlled scenarios

Less:
- spontaneous conversation
- informal dialogue

---

## 3. Audio Quality Issues
- overlapping speech in some segments
- background noise in longer recordings
- early transcription inconsistencies

---

## 4. Phonetic Constraints
IPA is:
- approximate
- not acoustically verified
- partially corrected after feedback

---

## 5. Analytical Limits
Some lexical distinctions (e.g. cook verbs) remain uncertain due to:
- limited tokens
- context dependency
